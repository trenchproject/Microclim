This platform visually compares different datasets to obtain microclimate data that are out there to facilitate the selection of the suitable data you are looking for.

The datasets we compare in this app are as follows.
  • USCRN - US Climate Reference Network
  • ERA5-Land
  • GLDAS 2.1 - Global Land Data Assimilation System
  • gridMET
  • NOAA NCDC - NOAA National Climatic Data Center
  • microclimUS - publication by Kearney et al. (2014)
  • microclim - publication by Kearney (2019)
  • SCAN - Soil Climate Analysis Network
